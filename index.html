Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

A leader who comes once in 100 years

Mustafa Kemal Atatürk at the parliamentary chair.

Here's the time line of Atatürk's life

  • 1881 - Mustafa was born in Thessaloniki.
  • 1893 - Mustafa starts the Military Preparatory School in Thessaloniki, where he is given the middle name "Kemâl" by his teacher.
  • 1895 - Mustafa Kemal starts the Military High School in Manastır.
  • 1899 - Mustafa Kemal starts the preparatory class at the Harbiye in Istanbul.
  • 1902 - Mustafa Kemal graduated from the Military Academy and then continued to the Military Academy.
  • 1905 - Mustafa Kemal graduated from the Military Academy as a Staff Captain and was sent to Damascus to serve in the Fifth Army in Damascus.
  • 1906 - Mustafa Kemâl ve arkadaşları Şam'da "Vatan ve Hürriyet" adıyla gizli bir dernek kurarlar.
  • 1907 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the Third Army and sent to Thessaloniki..
  • 1911 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the General Staff in Istanbul.
  • 1912 - Mustafa Kemal successfully leads the Tobruk offensive in Libya.
  • 1913 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed to Sofia as Military Attaché.
  • 1915 - The Allied Powers land in Arıburnu, and they stop their advance with the Mustafa Kemal Division.
  • 1916 - Mustafa Kemal is promoted to Brigadier General.
  • 1916 - Mustafa Kemal takes Bitlis and Muş back from the enemy.
  • 1918 - Mustafa Kemal becomes the Group Commander of the Yildirim Armies.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed as an Inspector to the Ninth Army in Erzurum with wide powers.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal sets foot in Samsun.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal is brought to the Presidency of Erzurum Congress.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal is brought to the Presidency of Sivas Congress.
  • 1920 - Mustafa Kemal opens the Turkish Grand National Assembly in Ankara.
  • 1921 - Mustafa Kemal is appointed by the Grand National Assembly as the Commander-in-Chief.
  • 1921 - The Battle of Sakarya, in which Turkish troops are led by Mustafa Kemal, begins.
  • 1921 - The TBMM gives Mustafa Kemal the rank of Marshal and the title of Gazi.
  • 1922 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal begins to lead the Great Offensive from Kocatepe.
  • 1922 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal Pasha wins the battle of Dumlupınar.
  • 1922 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal enters Izmir.
  • 1922 - The Grand National Assembly of Turkey accepts Gazi Mustafa Kemal's proposal to abolish the Caliphate.
  • 1923 - Mustafa Kemal's mother, Zübeyde Hanım, passed away in İzmir.
  • 1923 - The Republic of Turkey is proclaimed and Gazi Mustafa Kemal is elected as the first President.
  • 1924 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal wears a hat for the first time in Istanbul Sarayburnu.
  • 1928 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal gives a speech on the new Turkish Alphabet in Sarayburnu.
  • 1931 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal establishes the Turkish Historical Society.
  • 1932 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal establishes the Turkish Language Institution.
  • 1934 - The Turkish Grand National Assembly gave Gazi Mustafa Kemal the surname "Atatürk".
  • 1938 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal Atatürk dies.
